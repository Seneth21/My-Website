| The Sweet Story of "Rose |

"Rose" Artwork done for Pradeep Madahinne Original song, draws inspiration from the classic novel "Little House on the Prairie". The background, with its blend of classic European architecture and serene countryside, suggests a story that bridges different worlds and experiences. The overall composition, framed with a torn-edge effect, adds an additional layer of dimension, drawing the viewer into this introspective narrative. The artwork is a striking fusion of surrealism and realism and reflect on the meaning behind the title "Rose" and the narrative possibilities of the girl's journey.
